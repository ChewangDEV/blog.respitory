The Pirate King’s Journal: My Journey to Freedom

Monkey D. Luffy

Yo! This is Monkey D. Luffy, and if you're reading this, it means I’ve taken a
break from eating meat (which is rare) to write something down. I don’t
usually do this, but hey, being the future Pirate King
means sharing my adventures, right?

A Dream Worth Chasing

Ever since I was a kid, I knew I wanted to be the Pirate King. Not because of gold or treasure, but because it means I’ll be the freest person in the world. If you have a dream, don’t let anyone tell you it’s impossible. Just keep going, no matter how tough it gets!

A Crew Stronger Than Any Treasure

My nakama (crew) are everything to me! Whether it’s Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook, or Jinbe, each of them has a dream just like me, and I’ll fight anyone who tries to take that away from them! If you have people who believe in you, never let them go.

The Open Sea is Where I Belong

Nothing beats the feeling of sailing into the unknown! I don’t care about rules or what the Navy says—I’ll go wherever I want, do what I want, and live free! The world is huge, and I won’t stop until I’ve seen it all.
